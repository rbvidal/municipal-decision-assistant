QUARTERLY FINANCIAL REPORT — Q2 2026

Organization: Acme Corporation
Prepared by: Dr. Sarah Chen, Chief Financial Officer
Date: June 30, 2026

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Acme Corporation reported revenue of $45.2 million for Q2 2026, representing 12% year-over-year growth. Operating expenses were $18.7 million, with net income reaching $8.3 million after accounting for a one-time technology investment of $2.1 million in the Enterprise AI Platform developed by Cognitera Engineering.

- General & Administrative: $3.4 million

KEY METRICS

- Operating Margin: 18.4%
