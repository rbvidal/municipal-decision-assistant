Building Permit Procedure in Berlin. Step 1: Determine the applicable procedure type. Step 2: Engage a building document author (architect or civil engineer). Step 3: Prepare all required building documents including location plan, construction drawings, building description, structural analysis, and fire safety certificate. Step 4: Submit application electronically to the responsible district building authority. Step 5: Authority checks completeness within 4 weeks. Step 6: Neighbour participation per Section 70 BauO Bln. Step 7: Involvement of relevant authorities such as fire department, monument protection, and environmental agency. Step 8: Decision within 1 month after completeness confirmation. Step 9: Building permit is issued or denied in writing.
