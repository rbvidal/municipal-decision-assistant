Internal Berlin Procurement Order regulates purchasing within the administration. Individual purchases up to 500 euros may be made without prior approval from the supervisor. Purchases from 500 to 1,000 euros require written supervisor approval before ordering. Purchases over 1,000 euros require a formal procurement memorandum (Vergabevermerk) documenting the procurement decision, market research, and selection criteria. Three comparison offers are required for purchases over 500 euros for direct awards. The procurement memorandum must include justification for the chosen procedure and documentation of the offer evaluation. Budget responsibility lies with the respective cost center manager.
