The Federal Travel Expense Act (BRKG) governs reimbursement for official travel. Daily subsistence allowance within Germany: 6 euros for absences over 8 hours, 12 euros for over 11 hours, 24 euros for a full 24-hour day. Arrival and departure day with overnight stay: 12 euros. International rates vary by country. For example, Brussels: 47 euros per day. Mileage reimbursement for private car: 0.35 euros per kilometer. Lodging: up to 80 euros per night with receipt, 20 euros flat rate without receipt. Breakfast deduction: 2.17 euros in 2024, lunch or dinner: 4.13 euros each. Claim deadline: 6 months after travel completion. Public transport: 2nd class rail, economy class flights only with justification. After 3 months at the same external duty station, tax-free per diem eligibility expires.
