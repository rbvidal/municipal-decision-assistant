The Berlin Working Time Regulation implements the Federal Working Time Act (ArbZG). Regular weekly working time: 40 hours for civil servants, 39 hours 50 minutes for tariff employees. Flexitime: core hours are typically 9:30 to 15:00. Outside core hours, employees may choose their start and end times. Maximum daily working time: 10 hours. Rest period between work days: minimum 11 hours. Overtime must be approved in advance by the supervisor. Time credits can be carried forward to the following month up to a maximum of 40 hours. Part-time requests must be submitted 4 months before the desired start date for family reasons.
