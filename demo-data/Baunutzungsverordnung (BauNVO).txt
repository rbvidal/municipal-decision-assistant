The Land Use Ordinance (BauNVO) defines 10 building zone categories. Pure residential areas (WR) allow only residential use with a GRZ of 0.4 and GFZ of 1.2. General residential areas (WA) permit some commercial uses, GRZ 0.4, GFZ 1.2. Mixed areas (MI) allow residential and commercial in equal measure, GRZ 0.6, GFZ 1.2. Commercial areas (GE) are for non-hazardous businesses, GRZ 0.8, GFZ 2.4. Industrial areas (GI) permit all industrial uses, GRZ 0.8, GFZ 2.4. Special areas (SO) are designated for specific uses like retail, hospitals, or recreation.
