# Building & Urban Planning Domain — Bauen & Stadtplanung Berlin

**Domain 2 von 3 — Municipal Decision Assistant SCCON Demo**

## Scope

This domain covers building regulation and urban planning in Berlin:

- Berlin Building Code (BauO Bln) and associated ordinances
- Federal building and planning law (BauGB, BauNVO)
- Building permit procedures (full, simplified, exemption, type approval)
- Zoning and land-use planning (FNP, Bebauungspläne)
- Citizen information for builders and property owners
- Forms, checklists, and document requirements
- Recent reforms: Schneller-Bauen-Gesetz (Dec 2024), BauVorlV (Jun 2025)

## Document Count: ~30

- Senatsverwaltung für Stadtentwicklung, Bauen und Wohnen Berlin
- Bezirksämter (12 Bezirksämter) — Bauaufsichtsbehörden
- BMWSB (Federal Ministry for Housing, Urban Development and Building)
- Landesdenkmalamt Berlin
- Berliner Feuerwehr (Brandschutzdienststelle)

## Most Important Documents

1. **BauO Bln** — Berlin's building code; the single most important document
2. **BauVorlV 2025** — Building documents ordinance (NEW, replacing BauVerfV)
3. **BauGB** — Federal building code; legal basis for all planning
4. **BauNVO** — Land use ordinance; defines what can be built where
5. **Schneller-Bauen-Gesetz** — Recent reform accelerating procedures

## Key 2024/2025 Changes

- **BauVorlV 2025** replaces BauVerfV 2017 (effective 30.06.2025)
- **Typengenehmigung** (§ 72a BauO Bln) is now procedurally regulated
- **Bauantragskonferenz** mandatory for large projects (≥50 WE, schools, Kitas, >3.000 m²)
- **Qualifizierter Freiflächenplan** new requirement for larger developments
- **Electronic submission** standardized (PDF/A, naming convention JJJJMMTT)
- **4-week completeness check** + 1-month decision after completeness (Zustimmungsfiktion)
- **45 Norms changed** across 10 state laws + 69 sub-legal measures
