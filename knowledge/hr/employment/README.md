# HR & Internal Administration Domain — Personal & Innere Verwaltung Berlin

**Domain 3 von 3 — Municipal Decision Assistant SCCON Demo**

## Scope

This domain covers the internal administrative and human resources operations of Berlin municipal administration:

- Travel expense law and business travel (BRKG, LRKG Berlin)
- Vacation and leave regulations
- Home office and mobile work policies
- Working time regulations and flexitime
- Internal procurement approval processes
- IT security policies and data protection
- Employment law (TV-L collective agreement, pay tables, classification)
- Remuneration (salary tables, allowances, special payments)
- Internal administrative procedures (file management, signature rules)
