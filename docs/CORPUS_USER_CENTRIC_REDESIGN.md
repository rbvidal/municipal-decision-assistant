## Part 1 — A Day at the Desk

### 08:00 — Bauamt (Bauaufsicht), Bezirksamt Spandau

Frau Schröder arrives. Her computer boots. She has 23 active building permit cases.

**08:05** — Opens the case management system. Three new Bauanträge arrived yesterday. She opens the first: Einfamilienhaus, Berlin-Staaken. She reaches for the **Checkliste Bauantrag Einfamilienhaus** — a laminated A4 sheet next to her keyboard that she's used thousands of times. She checks: Lageplan vorhanden? Ja. Bauzeichnungen? Ja. Baubeschreibung? Ja. Statik? **Fehlt.**

She opens the **BauVorlV 2025 §2 Dokumentenliste** on the intranet to confirm: yes, Statik is required. She drafts an email to the applicant using the **Standardantwort fehlende Unterlagen (Muster)** template. Three minutes. Done.

**08:45** — Second application: a citizen wants to build a carport. Frau Schröder opens the **Merkblatt Genehmigungsfreie Vorhaben** on the intranet. She skims to "Carports bis 30 m² — genehmigungsfrei." She checks the application: 24 m², Innenbereich. She writes a positive Vorbescheid using the **Vorbescheid-Muster Genehmigungsfreiheit (Vorlage).**

**09:30** — Third case: a neighbor has complained about a new building being too close to the property line. Frau Schröder opens the **Abstandsflächen Leitfaden** on the intranet. 0.4 H, minimum 3 m. She checks the building plans: the new building is 8 m high, 2.8 m from the property line. Required: 3.2 m. Violation. She opens the **Drittwiderspruch-Prüfschema** decision tree: valid complaint → Anhörung → mögliche Baueinstellung. She drafts an Anhörung using the **Anhörungsschreiben Nachbar (Muster).**

**11:00** — A colleague from the Vergabestelle calls: "We're planning a small renovation of the Bauamt office. Is that a Bauantrag or genehmigungsfrei?" Frau Schröder opens the **FAQ Bauamt Intern** and finds: "Bauliche Änderungen in Bestandsgebäuden der öffentlichen Verwaltung — §61 BauO Bln, genehmigungsfrei wenn keine Statikänderung." She emails the FAQ link.

**13:00** — After lunch, she processes a Nutzungsänderung. A former bakery is becoming a café. She opens the **Leitfaden Nutzungsänderung** on the intranet, then the **Gebührenverzeichnis Bauamt** to calculate the fee: 250 €. She fills out the **Genehmigungsbescheid mit Auflagen (Muster)** template, adding the specific Auflagen for gastronomy (Lüftung, Brandschutz, Hygiene).

**14:30** — A building inspector reports from a field visit: a construction site is missing required fire safety signage. Frau Schröder opens the **Baueinstellungsverfügung (Muster)** template, customizes it for fire safety violations, and issues it.

**15:45** — End of day. She closes her 23 cases. Today she consulted:
- 0 copies of the GWB, VgV, or BRKG (not her domain)
- 0 copies of the actual BauO Bln law text (she knows it by heart after 20 years)
- 1 checklist (Bauantrag Einfamilienhaus)
- 2 templates (Standardantwort fehlende Unterlagen, Vorbescheid Genehmigungsfreiheit)
- 2 Leitfäden (Abstandsflächen, Nutzungsänderung)
- 1 FAQ document
- 1 fee schedule
- 1 decision tree (Drittwiderspruch)
- 2 Musterbescheide (Anhörung, Baueinstellung)
- 0 law texts

### 08:00 — Vergabestelle, Senatsverwaltung für Finanzen

Herr Yildirim arrives. He has 7 active procurement cases.

**08:15** — A Fachbereich needs 15 new laptops. Estimated value: 18.000 €. Herr Yildirim opens the **Vergabehandbuch Berlin, Kapitel 2: Beschränkte Ausschreibung** on the intranet. He reads the step-by-step guide: "1. Leistungsbeschreibung erstellen. 2. Angebotsaufforderung an mindestens 5 Bieter. 3. Frist: 14 Tage. 4. Angebote auswerten. 5. Vergabevermerk dokumentieren." He opens the **Checkliste Beschränkte Ausschreibung** and works through it.

**09:30** — He starts drafting the Vergabevermerk. He opens the **Vergabevermerk-Muster Beschränkte Ausschreibung (Vorlage)** and fills it in. The template has all required sections pre-labeled. He just fills in the project-specific details.

**10:45** — A colleague asks: "Can I buy a monitor for 450 € without a Vergabevermerk?" Herr Yildirim opens the **Rundschreiben Direktaufträge 2025** on the intranet: "Aufträge bis 500 € ohne Vergleichsangebote und ohne Vergabevermerk." He sends the link.

**13:00** — A bidder has filed a Rüge (complaint) about the evaluation. Herr Yildirim opens the **Leitfaden Rügen und Nachprüfungsverfahren** on the intranet. He checks: Rüge within 10 days? Yes. Rüge is substantiated? The evaluation matrix was unclear. He opens the **Rügebeantwortung (Muster)** template and drafts a response. He also opens the **Bewertungsmatrix-Muster Öffentliche Ausschreibung (Vorlage)** to revise the unclear evaluation criteria for the next round.

**15:00** — A new circular from SenFin arrives by email: "Wertgrenzen 2026 unverändert." Herr Yildirim reads it, notes the confirmation, and files it. He will reference this circular the next time a colleague asks about thresholds.

Today he consulted:
- 0 copies of the GWB or VgV (hasn't opened either in 5 years)
- 1 manual chapter (Vergabehandbuch Kap. 2)
- 2 checklists
- 1 template (Vergabevermerk)
- 1 circular (Direktaufträge 2025)
- 1 Leitfaden (Rügen)
- 1 Muster (Rügebeantwortung)

### 08:00 — Personalamt, Bezirksamt Mitte

Frau Nguyen arrives. 12 HR cases today.

**08:30** — A new colleague starts next month. Frau Nguyen opens the **Checkliste Einstellung neuer Mitarbeiter** and works through it: Arbeitsvertrag vorbereiten, Personalakte anlegen, IT-Zugang beantragen, Schlüssel bestellen, Onboarding-Termin vereinbaren. She opens the **Arbeitsvertrag TV-L (Muster)** and fills in the new employee's details: EG 9b Stufe 2.

**10:00** — An employee requests to carry over 5 vacation days to next year. Frau Nguyen opens the **FAQ Urlaub und Sonderurlaub** and finds: "Resturlaub kann bis 31.03. des Folgejahres übertragen werden bei dringenden betrieblichen Gründen (§26 Abs. 4 TV-L)." She opens the **UrlVO Bln** on the intranet to verify the Berlin-specific rules, then approves the carryover using the **Urlaubsübertragung Genehmigung (Muster).**

**11:30** — An employee wants to switch to 4-day week. Frau Nguyen opens the **Leitfaden Teilzeitanträge** and the **Teilzeit-Rechner (Excel)** to calculate the new salary. She opens the **Teilzeitantrag (Muster)** and the **Änderungsvertrag TV-L (Muster).**

**14:00** — A new job posting needs to be written. Frau Nguyen opens the **Stellenbeschreibung (Muster)** and the **Checkliste Stellenausschreibung.** She also opens the **TV-L Entgeltordnung** to verify the correct Entgeltgruppe for "Sachbearbeiterin Bauaufsicht mit Berufserfahrung."

**15:30** — An employee submits travel expenses from a conference in Leipzig. Frau Nguyen opens the **Dienstreise-Abrechnung (Muster)** and the **BRKG Inlandstagegeld Tabelle.** She verifies: 12 hours away → 12 € Tagegeld, 80 € Hotel mit Beleg. She processes the reimbursement.

Today she consulted:
- 0 copies of the TV-L law text
- 3 checklists (Einstellung, Stellenausschreibung, Dienstreise)
- 4 templates (Arbeitsvertrag, Teilzeitantrag, Urlaubsübertragung, Dienstreise-Abrechnung)
- 2 FAQs
- 2 Leitfäden
- 1 Entgeltordnung (TV-L Entgeltordnung)
- 1 fee/reference table (BRKG Tagegeld)

**15:45** — A citizen asks: "Can I get a Beglaubigung for this document?" Herr Öztürk opens the **FAQ Beglaubigungen** to confirm: yes, copies can be certified for 5 € per page. He processes it.

## Part 2 — Document Types by Actual Usage

| Rank | Document Type | Usage Frequency | Example | Why It's Used |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Checklists** | **Every day, many times** | Checkliste Direktauftrag | "Did I forget anything?" The #1 tool for preventing errors. |
| 2 | **Templates / Muster** | **Every day** | Vergabevermerk-Vorlage | "Just fill in the blanks." Reduces document creation from 30 min to 5 min. |
| 3 | **Fee schedules / Gebührenverzeichnisse** | **Every day** | Gebührenverzeichnis Bürgeramt | "How much does this service cost?" Most common citizen question. |
| 4 | **FAQs** | **Every day** | FAQ Urlaub und Sonderurlaub | "Someone asked this before. The answer already exists." |
| 5 | **Internal process descriptions** | **Every day** | Leitfaden Bauantragstellung | "What are the steps for this?" Step-by-step beats reading the law. |
| 6 | **Manuals / Handbücher** | **Every week** | Vergabehandbuch Berlin | The comprehensive reference for a whole area of work. |
| 7 | **Administrative regulations (AVs)** | **Every week** | AV §55 LHO | "What does the administration actually require?" More specific than law. |
| 8 | **Circulars (Rundschreiben)** | **Every week** | Rundschreiben Direktaufträge 2025 | "What is the CURRENT interpretation?" Law may be years old; circulars are current. |
| 9 | **Forms (Formulare)** | **Every week** | Bauantragsformular | The official form that citizens must use. |
| 10 | **Decision trees / Prüfschemata** | **Every week** | Drittwiderspruch-Prüfschema | "If X, then Y. If not X, then Z." Structured logic. |
| 11 | **Laws (Gesetze)** | **Every month** | BauO Bln | Consulted for specific, unusual questions. Not read cover-to-cover. |
| 12 | **Regulations (Rechtsverordnungen)** | **Every month** | BauVorlV 2025 | Consulted together with laws. |
| 13 | **Court decisions** | **Every month** (Vergabestelle only) | Vergabekammer-Leitsatzentscheidung | "Has this situation been tested in court?" Risk management. |
| 14 | **Collective agreements (Tarifverträge)** | **Every month** | TV-L Entgelttabellen | Salary and classification questions. |
| 15 | **Training materials** | **Every quarter** | Onboarding-Handbuch | When new employees start. |
| 16 | **Historical versions** | **Almost never** | BauO Bln 2018 | Only when a case requires the law as it stood historically. |

The legal foundation. Important but consulted far less often than operational documents.

## Part 7 — Future Vision: 5,000 Daily Users

When 5,000 Berlin municipal employees use this platform every day, the corpus that makes them say **"I don't need to search the intranet anymore"** contains:

**7,500 documents**, not 300. Organized as:

- **2,500 operational documents** (checklists, templates, FAQs, process descriptions, fee schedules, decision trees, sample forms, standard responses). Every procedure in every Amt has a checklist. Every standard decision has a template. Every common citizen question has a FAQ entry. Every fee is documented. Every workflow step is described. **This is the daily toolset.**

- **2,000 administrative regulations and circulars** (AVs, Dienstvereinbarungen, Rundschreiben, Erlasse). Every Berlin-specific rule, every current interpretation, every departmental policy. Updated within 48 hours of publication. **This is the institutional memory.**

- **1,500 laws, regulations, and EU directives** (complete texts, current versions, with §-level citations). The complete legal foundation. Searchable, citable, versioned. **This is the legal backbone.**

- **1,000 court decisions** (all Vergabekammer Berlin decisions, all OVG Berlin-Brandenburg Baurecht decisions, key BVerwG and EuGH decisions). Annotated with Leitsätze and affected regulations. **This is the interpretive layer.**

- **500 forms and form instructions** (every official form, with line-by-line instructions for how to fill it out). Available in German, English, Turkish, Arabic. **This is the citizen interface.**

This is the corpus of a platform that has replaced the intranet, the filing cabinet, the colleague with 20 years of experience, and the Google search for "BauO Bln §61."

The current corpus has 28 documents. We are designing the first 100-300. The vision is 7,500. Every 100 documents added makes the intranet less necessary.
